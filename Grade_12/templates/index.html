Welcome to Year 12,
Sirius College

Year 12 students are ambitious young individuals preparing for their transition into adulthood and higher education. On this website, they explore resources to support their academic growth, career aspirations, and personal development. Whether they are researching universities, preparing for exams, or seeking guidance on life after graduation, these students are focused on achieving their goals. They are driven, tech-savvy, and ready to take on new challenges as they approach the next chapter of their educational journey.

Curriculum

Year 12, the final year of secondary education and the culmination of the Victorian Certificate of Education (VCE), follows the pivotal Year 11, setting the stage for future opportunities and pathways. With a focus on consolidating knowledge and skills, Year 12 holds a profound influence on university admissions and career prospects. This year carries the weight of academic expectations and calls for a disciplined approach to study, time management, and effective exam preparation. By embracing the significance of Year 12, seeking guidance from educators and mentors, and engaging in focused and purposeful learning, students can maximise their potential, achieve outstanding results, and open doors to a bright and successful future beyond secondary school.

Co-curricular

Sirius College offers a variety of co-curricular activities that enhance students' learning experience outside the classroom. These include:
  • Sports and Athletics: Students can engage in different sports like soccer, tennis, and more.
  • Clubs: Sirius College offers various student-led clubs that cater to interests such as debate, arts, and leadership.
  • Community Service: Volunteering and service projects are encouraged, fostering social responsibility.
  • Cultural Events: Students participate in cultural celebrations that promote diversity and inclusivity.
  • Leadership Programs: Workshops and seminars are provided to help students develop leadership skills.
These activities help students grow in both personal and academic spheres.

Events

Sirius College offers several key events throughout the school year that provide opportunities for student engagement and community building. Some notable events include:
  • STEM Competitions: Students represent the school in science and technology challenges, like robotics contests.
  • Cultural Celebrations: Annual events that celebrate cultural diversity and inclusivity across the student body.
  • Sports Days: A highlight for students who participate in inter-house and inter-school athletic competitions.
  • VCE Graduation Ceremony: A major event celebrating the achievements of students completing their Victorian Certificate of Education (VCE).
  • Music and Drama Performances: Students showcase their artistic talents in concerts and school plays.
These events contribute to a vibrant school community at Sirius College.
